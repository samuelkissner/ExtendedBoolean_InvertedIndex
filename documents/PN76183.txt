<TITLE>Airline travel for children with chronic pulmonary disease.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>Air travel for children with chronic pulmonary disease is usually safe, but the potential hazards must be considered. The high altitude, obviously, is that which provides the potential problem. Before specifically relating the problem to the children, a few words are in order concerning airline procedures and policies.</ABSTRACT>
