<TITLE>Cystic fibrosis in a patient with Kartagener syndrome.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>A patient exhibited the features of both Kartagener syndrome and cystic fibrosis. At most, to the authors' knowledge, this represents the third such report of the combination. Cystic fibrosis should be excluded before a diagnosis of Kartagener syndrome is made.</ABSTRACT>
