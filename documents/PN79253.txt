<TITLE>Implications of cystic fibrosis for health services and the afflicted.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>"Psychosocial" literature on cystic fibrosis (CF) is critically reviewed. Major methodological shortcomings include the anecdotal nature of findings, overemphasis on negative consequences of the disease, and the lack of a suitable theoretical framework. Avenues for future research are suggested.</ABSTRACT>
