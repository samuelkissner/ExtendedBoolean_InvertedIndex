<TITLE>Iodide-induced hypothyroidism without a goiter in an infant with cystic fibrosis.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>It has been suggested that patients with cystic fibrosis may have an intrinsic abnormality of thyroid function that may be enhanced by the administration of iodides. We wish to report an infant with CF in whom hyperthyroidism developed, in the absence of a goiter, following short-term iodide therapy.</ABSTRACT>
