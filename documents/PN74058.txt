<TITLE>Glucose intolerance in a three-year-old child with cystic fibrosis.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>Glucose intolerance preceding pulmonary disease in a child with cystic fibrosis is described. After glucose intolerance was controlled with tolbutamide, the pulmonary infection resolved. Early recognition and treatment of "diabetes" in children with cystic fibrosis is emphasized.</ABSTRACT>
