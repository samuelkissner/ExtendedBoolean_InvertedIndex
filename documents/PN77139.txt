<TITLE>Multilobular biliary cirrhosis of the liver associated with cystic fibrosis (mucoviscidosis).</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>A case (including autopsy findings) is reported of multilobular biliary cirrhosis with a strictly nodular liver developing in a 20- year-old male with cystic fibrosis (mucoviscidosis). The pathogenesis of biliary cirrhosis in cystic fibrosis is briefly discussed.</ABSTRACT>
