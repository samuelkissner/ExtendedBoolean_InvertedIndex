<TITLE>Cystic fibrosis: a review of clinic patients.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>The response of 72 patients to an intensive antimicrobial prophylactic programme is presented in this review. Treatment was first commenced in 1968 and was evaluated during the last 4 months of 1975. The assessment confirmed a favourable clinical impression.</ABSTRACT>
