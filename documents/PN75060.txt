<TITLE>Cystic fibrosis.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>Cystic fibrosis, whether it is called fibrocystic disease of the pancreas, mucoviscidosis, of simply C.F., is a condition that currently afflicts one in every 2,500 children. A glimpse of this disease is gained through the experience of Amelia, one of its victims.</ABSTRACT>
