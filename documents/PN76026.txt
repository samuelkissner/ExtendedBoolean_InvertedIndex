<TITLE>Is cystic fibrosis an acid mucopolysaccharidosis?.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>A case of cystic fibrosis with myocardial fibrosis and acid mucopolysaccharide deposits in the myocardium is described. The case supports the theory that cystic fibrosis may be a genetic acid mucopolysaccharidosis.</ABSTRACT>
