<TITLE>Round table discussion--treatment of lower respiratory tract infections with co-trimoxazole.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>Over one thousand patients with acute and chronic chest infections have been treated with a combination of trimethoprim and sulphamethoxazole. The patients may be divided into four groups and the results are summarised in this presentation. Much of the work is published elsewhere.</ABSTRACT>
