<TITLE>Intrapancreatic compression of the common bile duct in cystic fibrosis.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>We report an infant with cystic fibrosis presenting at birth with meconium ileus who developed prolonged cholestatic jaundice from compression of the common bile duct and the pancreatic duct by advanced pancreatic fibrosis. Extrahepatic biliary obstruction of this nature has not been previously described in cystic fibrosis.</ABSTRACT>
