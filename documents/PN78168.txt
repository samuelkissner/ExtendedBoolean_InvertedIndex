<TITLE>Childhood sarcoidosis with fatal cor pulmonale.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>A 14 year-old white girl who developed fatal cor pulmonale from severe pulmonary sarcoidosis had a previous clinical diagnosis of cystic fibrosis. Chest radiographs demonstrated marked pulmonary fibrosis without the changes of severe bronchiectasis that would be anticipated with cystic fibrosis.</ABSTRACT>
