<TITLE>Cystic fibrosis.</TITLE>
<ABSTRACT>Cystic fibrosis has been regarded as a disease of infants. Yet, with improved management, a high percentage of patients how survive into young adulthood. In addition, mild cases may not be detected until long after infance. The older patient often has minimal symptoms, and the majority are socially active and productive.</ABSTRACT>
